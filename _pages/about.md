## About

I am currently a **CPU Architect at Arm, Cambridge, UK**.

Before joining Arm, I worked as a **Senior Research Scientist at Technology Innovation Institute, Abu Dhabi**. Prior to that, I worked as a **Senior Engineer at Qualcomm**.

My work focuses on building high-performance, secure, and efficient computing systems, with interests spanning CPU architecture, microarchitecture, hardware security, memory systems, edge AI, GPU performance, and AI-assisted architecture design.
